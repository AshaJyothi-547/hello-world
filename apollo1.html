Enabling your business to lend

Build the perfect financial product for your customers using your own proprietary data. Some of our products include short term/long term personal loans, consumer financing, checkout financing, pay later services, settlement financing, business loans. Our modular approach enables your business

We treat the NBFC services on our platform as building blocks, which our partners can mix and match to create banking services specifically tailored to their business model. These building blocks include the Lending license, Compliance, Regulation, Loan Reporting, Accessing credit reports, Digital KYC, Fraud detection, Loan disbursements, Payment gateways, Bank statement analysis, Access to telecom data for alternate underwriting, Loan Management Systems, Purchasing bad loans- all accessible via APIs.
